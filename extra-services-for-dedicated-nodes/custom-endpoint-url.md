# Custom Endpoint URL

The custom endpoint URL lets you own your URL for your dedicated node. Think of this like a custom domain name that reflects your brand name. This replaces the auto-generated hash with a URL you actually chose.

By default, your endpoint holds a random access token in the path, for example `https://go.getblock.io/<ACCESS-TOKEN>/`. This add-on lets you serve the same node from a URL you control, for example `https://rpc.yourapp.com`.

### How it works

* You point a domain you own at your GetBlock node with a CNAME record.
* Or you choose a readable subdomain in place of the hash.
* You keep your authentication in a header, such as `x-access-token`, instead of in the URL. A URL with a secret in it leaks the secret in logs and browser history.
* The custom URL hides the infrastructure provider from your public config.

### Use cases

* You build a white-label product, and you want URLs to carry your brand.
* You do not want your public config to name your provider.
* You want a stable, readable URL that does not change when you rotate keys.
* For easy referencing&#x20;

### When you can skip it

* Your endpoint is internal and no user sees it.
* The default URL already meets your needs.
